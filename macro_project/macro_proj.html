Welcome to KSCT Group

We Provide Innovative Education
across the India


KSCT, as one of th largest chain of Educational Institutions under the ethical Patronage of its Founder Chairman "Acharya Shri Sudarshan Jee Maharaj" an Educationist turned Spritual Guru.KSCT belives in the fusion of Indian origin values with modern educational technology to resume the stress free education for the next generations of the globe.

KSCT (Krishna Sudarshan Charitabule Trust)is an innovative learning organization created to improve the quality of education in India. Established in 2008 to provide education to poor children across the India, KSCT has grown in both scope and scale, with programs today reaching children and youth across the country.
